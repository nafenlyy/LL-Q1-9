Problems facing Sitio Bebe:

  1. Closing of mines and factories - Mines and factories began to temporarily close, resulting in job losses and economic difficulties for residents.

  2. Lack of Access to Clean Water - The community is not near a natural water source, residents must rely on the local government to import clean water or dig open wells.

  3. Lack of employees in training centers - The onset of the health crisis reduced the number of people attending training centers, which could have otherwise provided residents with opportunities to learn new skills and improve their livelihoods.

  4. Pollution - While the community has access to valuable natural resources such as coal and minerals, over-reliance on these resources for livelihoods can lead to environmental degradation and reliance on markets that are unstable.
